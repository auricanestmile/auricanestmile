<h2>Basic Troubleshooting</h2>
<p><b>Issue: Everything is pitch black except for textures</b><br>
<b>Solution:</b> Change your renderer. Most people seem to have luck with Standard, but for some people, Performance or even Compatibility is better. You will need to restart.</p>
<p><b>Issue: Everything loads slow, my clothes don't show up</b><br>
<b>Solution:</b> Go to settings -&gt; advanced and set your asset queue to a higher number than the default 5. This game loads a LOT of assets. You will need to restart.</p>
<p><b>Issue: The game runs like total crap!</b><br>
<b>Solution:</b> 2.0 is like that. You can try to fix this by disabling almost all of the options in post-processing. The only one I'd say is truly visually important is Glow.</p>
